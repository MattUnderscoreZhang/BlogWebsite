# Life Calendar App

This calendar would let you see the progress of your life in weeks, as described in this post: https://waitbutwhy.com/2014/05/life-weeks.html.

I abandoned this project because it's already been done.

life calendar app
    prompt to enter one thing in each category each day
        what progress did you make towards a goal?
        what interesting experience did you have?
        misc notable event
    on each week, select one thing from each category as representative
        can add new item
    hover over blocks on calendars to see weeks or days
    views based on life, year, or month
    can pin current goal at top

Abandoned on 11/20/2022.
